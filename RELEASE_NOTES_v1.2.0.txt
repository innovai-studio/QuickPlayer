QuickPlayer v1.2.0 重大更新！

新功能：
• Focus EQ - 5 段預設音色強化（人聲、吉他、鼓、低音）+ 即時頻率響應曲線

• 節拍器 click 音量大幅提升,能蓋過大聲歌曲

延續功能：
• 速度調整 0.25x-2.0x、音調調整 ±12 半音
• A-B 段落循環、練習標記

<en-US>
QuickPlayer v1.2.0 Major Update!

New:
• Focus EQ — 5 instrument-tuned presets (Vocal / Guitar / Drums / Bass-) with live frequency-response curve
• Live spectrum analyser — see the song's per-band energy in real time
• Drag any band on the visualiser to tune your own EQ; saved per song
• Beat-aligned metronome — tap to sync, choose 2/4/3/4/4/4/6/8/12/8 time signatures
• Custom EQ memory per track

Improvements:
• Device-audio tracks now also get automatic BPM / Key analysis
• Finished tracks restart with one tap instead of two
• Metronome click is dramatically louder, cuts through loud songs

Continuing:
• Speed 0.25x–2.0x, pitch ±12 semitones
• A-B loop and practice markers
